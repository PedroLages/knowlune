# Gemini Logo Generation Prompts — Knowlune

Use these prompts with Google Gemini (or similar image AI) to explore visual logo variations. Each prompt builds on the established brand direction: geometric, modern, indigo crescent moon.

---

## Prompt 1: App Icon (Primary)

> Design a minimal, geometric app icon for "Knowlune", a personal learning platform. The icon features a crescent moon formed by two overlapping circles — the negative space creates a clean crescent shape. Use indigo (#5e6ad2) for the crescent on a near-black (#030213) background with rounded corners. The style should be modern and tech-forward, similar to Linear or Vercel icons. No text, just the geometric crescent mark. Square format, suitable for app stores and PWA install screens. High resolution, 512x512px.

## Prompt 2: Full Logo Lockup

> Create a horizontal logo for "Knowlune" (a learning platform). Left side: a geometric crescent moon icon in indigo (#5e6ad2) formed by overlapping circles. Right side: the word "Knowlune" in a clean geometric sans-serif font (similar to Space Grotesk), semi-bold weight, dark text. Below the wordmark in small caps: the tagline "ILLUMINATE YOUR PATH" in muted gray. White background. Modern, minimal aesthetic like Stripe or Linear branding. Flat design, no gradients or shadows.

## Prompt 3: Crescent Variations

> Generate 4 variations of a geometric crescent moon icon for an app called "Knowlune". All should use indigo (#5e6ad2) as the primary color. Variation 1: Classic crescent from two circles. Variation 2: Crescent with a subtle orbital ring suggesting a learning path. Variation 3: Crescent with a small star/dot accent suggesting illumination. Variation 4: Abstract crescent made from geometric segments/facets. All on dark (#030213) backgrounds with rounded-corner squares. Clean, minimal, tech-forward. Show all 4 side by side.

## Prompt 4: Branded Splash Screen

> Design a splash/loading screen for "Knowlune", a learning app. Center: a geometric indigo (#5e6ad2) crescent moon icon. Below it: "Knowlune" in clean sans-serif, then "Illuminate Your Path" as a smaller tagline. Background: warm off-white (#FAF5EE). The overall mood should be calm, aspirational, and modern. Minimal design, generous whitespace. Suitable for PWA loading screen.

## Prompt 5: Social Media / Marketing Banner

> Create a marketing banner for "Knowlune" learning platform. Features: geometric crescent moon logo in indigo on the left, "Knowlune" wordmark center, tagline "Illuminate Your Path" below. Background: gradient from warm off-white (#FAF5EE) to soft indigo tint. Modern, aspirational, clean typography. 1200x630px (Open Graph size). No stock photos, pure brand graphics.
